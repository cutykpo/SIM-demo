How it works

A clinical protocol, run by AI

1
Clinical intake

The AI agent asks about the resident's condition, mobility aids, fall history, and cognitive status — the same questions a specialist would ask before a home visit.

2
Guided photo upload

Upload 5 photos — entrance, bathroom, kitchen, bedroom, and hallways. The agent guides the angle and framing for each room based on the specific condition.

3
Gemini Vision analysis

Gemini 2.5 Pro analyzes each image against the clinical risk profile of the resident's condition. Every recommendation reflects condition-specific protocols.
